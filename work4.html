Seen Tide till Time

2025-09-20

A temporal art piece from my CC Lab archive, realized in p5.js. This work probes the paradox of the present moment through interactive chromatic interaction—as your cursor traverses the canvas, the visual field oscillates between absolute black and absolute white, each movement a meditation on time's relentless current. The piece asks: where does the now actually dwell? Between light and void, between what has already become memory and what remains perpetually deferred, the moment itself is elusive, forever slipping through consciousness's grasp. Time marches forward without hesitation, yet we desperately search for a fixed foothold in its stream. This work invites contemplation on whether we can ever truly inhabit the present, or if we are condemned to perpetually chase its shadow. Click the image to explore further.
