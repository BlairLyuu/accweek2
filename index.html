Yuxin Liu

Artist

Designer

Curator

I’m a bilingual creative with a growing passion for curatorial practice and the art market. Though I’m still at the beginning of my journey, I’ve been fortunate to gain early experience through an internship at a contemporary gallery in Xi’an, China. There, I contributed to exhibition label writing, supported daily operations, and managed the gallery’s Rednote account to reflect its youthful, public-oriented identity. By practicing empathetic communication and exploring what truly engages an audience, I’ve gained meaningful experience and continue to learn with each new opportunity. Currently, I’m majoring in Design and Technology at Parsons, and I’ll be starting a Museum and Curatorial Studies minor next semester. I’m excited to learn more about how a professional gallery works and hope to contribute with creativity, strong communication skills, and a willingness to grow.
