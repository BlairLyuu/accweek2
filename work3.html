Letter from an Unknown Woman

2024-12-20

This project reimagines the novel A Letter from an Unknown Woman as a contemporary stage-play poster, exploring the gap between visible glamour and inner emotional weight. In this adaptation, the poster is not just a promotional image, but a visual and emotional entry point into the story's deeper themes: unrequited love, spiritual longing, memory, and the haunting persistence of absence. Through careful composition and design, these works aim to communicate complex ideas in a visually engaging manner.
