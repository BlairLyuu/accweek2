The Distance Between us and Memory

2025-12-15

This project is a nostalgic exploration of Metrograph as a sanctuary for a newcomer to New York. By recreating the cinema's unique atmosphere—its wooden textures, warm lighting, and intimate bar—the game invites players to experience the "quiet growth" of becoming a New Yorker. It archives personal milestones through the lens of film, blending the ritual of movie-going with the search for belonging in a foreign city.
